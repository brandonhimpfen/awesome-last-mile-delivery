## Delivery Management Platforms

Platforms for managing delivery operations, dispatching, and workflows.

- [Onfleet](https://onfleet.com/) — Delivery management platform for last-mile logistics.
- [Bringg](https://www.bringg.com/) — Delivery orchestration platform for enterprise logistics.
- [FarEye](https://fareye.com/) — Last-mile delivery platform with real-time visibility and optimization.

## Route Optimization

Tools for planning efficient delivery routes and reducing operational costs.

- [OptimoRoute](https://optimoroute.com/) — Route planning and optimization platform.
- [Routific](https://routific.com/) — Route optimization tool for delivery businesses.
- [Route4Me](https://route4me.com/) — Routing software for logistics and delivery operations.

## Tracking & Customer Experience

Platforms enhancing visibility, tracking, and communication with customers.

- [Shipday](https://www.shipday.com/) — Delivery tracking and dispatch platform.
- [Tookan](https://www.jungleworks.com/tookan/) — Delivery management and tracking platform.
- [Project44](https://www.project44.com/) — Real-time logistics visibility platform.

## Fleet & Driver Management

Tools for managing drivers, vehicles, and operational efficiency.

- [Samsara](https://www.samsara.com/) — Fleet management and telematics platform.
- [Geotab](https://www.geotab.com/) — Fleet tracking and analytics solutions.
- [Verizon Connect](https://verizonconnect.com/) — Fleet and driver management platform.

## Autonomous & Emerging Delivery

Innovative technologies shaping the future of last-mile delivery.

- [Nuro](https://www.nuro.ai/) — Autonomous delivery vehicles for local goods transportation.
- [Starship Technologies](https://www.starship.xyz/) — Autonomous delivery robots for urban environments.
- [Zipline](https://www.flyzipline.com/) — Drone delivery system for medical and commercial logistics.

## Retail & E-commerce Delivery

Platforms and services focused on e-commerce and retail delivery operations.

- [DoorDash Drive](https://www.doordash.com/drive/) — White-label delivery service for businesses.
- [Uber Direct](https://www.uber.com/us/en/business/direct/) — On-demand delivery platform for retailers.
- [Instacart Platform](https://www.instacart.com/company/platform/) — Grocery delivery and fulfillment technology.
